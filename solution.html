HOW DOES IT WORK?

ECO HELPMATE is a free smartphone app for everyone who wants to be more conscious of their footprint.

The app makes it super easy to take climate-friendly action by helping the user pick sustainable options while shopping.

The app will be used to scan the barcode of any product, and the user will immediately be notified whether it is made by sustainable standards or not.

There are no obligations to enter any private information and it is solely an app to help the user pick the right choice for a better tomorrow.
